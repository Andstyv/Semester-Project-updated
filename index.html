Energetica Exhibition

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum.

It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power. Don't miss out on this extraordinary oppurtunity to experience some of the most exciting inventions for a sustainable and more eco-friendly future. Having been one of the most popular exhibitions in Amsterdam for over two years, we're more than excited to finally have the oppurtunity to showcase it for you. Come on down to our museum!
